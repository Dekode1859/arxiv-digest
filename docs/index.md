Automated daily digest of AI/LLM research papers from arXiv.

## About

This project fetches the latest papers from arXiv categories:
- **cs.AI** - Artificial Intelligence
- **cs.CL** - Computation and Language

Papers are then enriched with LLM-generated summaries, categorized by type (architecture, benchmark, method, dataset, application), and filtered to focus on:
- Agents & Agentic AI
- LLMs & Fine-tuning
- Benchmarking & Evaluation
- Reasoning
- Multi-agent systems
